Most searched questions (answered inside your result)

Why do I overthink everything in my relationship?

Because uncertainty is your trigger—your brain tries to create control by analyzing. The fix is not “think harder,” it’s clearer requests + nervous-system regulation.

How do I stop overthinking texts?

Decide a rule before you’re triggered: one check-in + a time boundary. Then shift to body calming (walk, breathing) so your brain stops treating silence as danger.

Is overthinking a sign of insecurity?

Often it’s a sign of safety needs. Sometimes it’s personal anxiety; sometimes it’s an inconsistent relationship. Your result helps you separate the two.

Is it intuition or anxiety?

Intuition is usually calm and specific. Anxiety is loud, urgent, and story-heavy. This test helps you spot which one is driving you.
