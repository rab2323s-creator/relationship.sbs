Are You Trauma Bonded in a Toxic Relationship? Take the 2-Minute Quiz to Find Out

Warning Signs of a Trauma Bond: Emotional Addiction, Intermittent Reinforcement, and Toxic Attachment Patterns

If leaving feels harder than staying — even when you're hurting — this test is for you.

A trauma bond doesn’t always look dramatic. It often looks like intense chemistry, followed by distance. Deep connection, followed by confusion. Warmth, followed by silence.

People searching for trauma bond test, emotional addiction in relationships, or toxic attachment patterns usually aren’t looking for drama. They’re trying to understand why something painful feels impossible to leave.

What This Trauma Bond Test Measures

  • Intermittent reinforcement patterns
  • Emotional dependency cycles
  • Attachment triggered by inconsistency
  • Difficulty leaving despite emotional distress

What It Doesn’t Do

  • This is not a diagnosis.
  • This does not label your partner.
  • This is not therapy or medical advice.

Your Results

You’ll receive one of five profiles explaining whether the attachment is healthy, intense-but-balanced, unstable, reinforcing dependency, or likely trauma-bond driven.
