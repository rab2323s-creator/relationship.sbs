Push–Pull Relationship Cycle — Hot and Cold Attachment Pattern

Push–pull dynamics create emotional intensity through unpredictability. Closeness is followed by distance, then closeness again. This pattern strengthens attachment through uncertainty.

Common Push–Pull Signs

  • Intense affection followed by withdrawal.
  • Frequent confusion after good moments.
  • Emotional highs and lows feel extreme.

Breaking the Cycle

  • Ask for predictable communication.
  • Do not chase during withdrawal.
  • Measure consistency over intensity.
