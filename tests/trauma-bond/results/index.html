Understanding Trauma Bonding, Emotional Addiction & Push-Pull Cycles

Trauma bonding isn’t about labels. It’s about patterns. Repeated emotional distress followed by intense relief can create powerful attachment loops. Below are the five possible profiles from your Trauma Bond Test.

  • Secure Attachment Dynamic
  • Intense Attachment Pattern
  • Push–Pull Relationship Cycle
  • Emotional Addiction Loop
  • Trauma Bond Pattern

Not a Diagnosis

These profiles are educational reflections based on common relationship dynamics. If distress feels severe or overwhelming, consider speaking with a licensed professional.
