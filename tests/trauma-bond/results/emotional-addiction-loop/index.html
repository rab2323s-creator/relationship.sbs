Emotional Addiction Loop — When Relief Feels Like Oxygen

This result suggests emotional dependency driven by relief cycles. The relationship may feel impossible to detach from because calm only returns when connection returns.

Signs of Emotional Addiction in a Relationship

  • Mood depends on their closeness.
  • Checking behaviors increase during distance.
  • Relief after affection feels intense and overwhelming.

What Helps

  • Create no-text pause windows.
  • Strengthen daily routines outside the relationship.
  • Build emotional regulation tools.
