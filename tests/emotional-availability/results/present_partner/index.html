Emotional Availability Test — Result Profile

Your result suggests a strong pattern of emotional availability. In practical terms, this tends to look like emotional presence, clearer communication, and repair after conflict—so emotional support feels real and consistent rather than dependent on mood, timing, or convenience.

What this result often means

  • Emotional support is consistent: empathy shows up even during stress
  • Clarity is available: “Are we okay?” leads to a real conversation, not vagueness
  • Repair happens: tension is followed by reconnection, not distance
  • Follow-through: caring words match repeatable actions over time

Green flags to notice (and protect)

  • You feel safer being honest—less “walking on eggshells”
  • Needs can be discussed without shame, guilt, or punishment
  • Accountability is possible without turning it into blame
  • Conflict doesn’t erase closeness; it gets repaired

Next steps

  • Keep a weekly check-in: 5–10 minutes, one “what worked” + one “what would help”
  • Use listening-first: “Do you want comfort, advice, or both?”
  • Agree on a repair rule: after conflict, reconnect within 24 hours

Quick scripts (ad-safe)

  • “I don’t need fixing—just presence. Can you reflect what you heard for 30 seconds?”
  • “Can we do a quick reset? I want us to reconnect after that moment.”
  • “What would help you feel supported this week—and what would help me?”
