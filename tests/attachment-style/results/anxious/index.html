The signature pattern

  • Uncertainty hits → alarm rises (mood shifts, scanning for signs).
  • Alarm rises → reassurance seeking (checking, asking, overexplaining, chasing clarity).
  • Reassurance works briefly, then the cycle repeats if the dynamic is inconsistent.

What this looks like in real moments

  • Slow replies: you read tone, timing, punctuation—anything to predict safety.
  • Mixed signals: your brain fills gaps with worst-case stories.
  • After closeness: you want momentum, confirmation, “where is this going?”
  • Conflict: urgency to fix now—because “unresolved” feels dangerous.

Your 3-step upgrade (practical, not preachy)

  1. Replace guessing with a timeline: “Can you text me when you’re busy, and when you’ll be free?”
  2. Regulate before you message: body first (walk, breath, cold water), then one clear sentence.
  3. Stop the spike behaviors: no checking “seen”, no rereading, no tests—ask directly or pause.
