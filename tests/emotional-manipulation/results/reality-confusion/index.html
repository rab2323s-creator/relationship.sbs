Reality Confusion Signals — Your Feelings Keep Getting Rewritten

What This Result Means

You may leave conversations feeling uncertain, guilty, or unsure whether your feelings are “valid.” The issue isn’t disagreement—it’s that your reality keeps becoming negotiable.

Common Patterns

  • Being told you’re “too sensitive” instead of being heard.
  • Detail debates that erase the bigger pattern.
  • Over-explaining to feel believed.

Why This Is Draining

When your emotions become a logic puzzle, self-trust erodes. Confusion becomes the cost of connection.

Helpful Scripts

  • “I’m not debating my feelings. I’m naming impact.”
  • “Let’s stay on one issue and one agreement.”

Next Steps

Document patterns privately. Set limits around circular conversations. Clarity is protection—not aggression.
