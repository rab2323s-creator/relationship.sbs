An independent platform with practical, research-informed content for healthier communication and boundaries.

Relationship.sbs is an independent educational platform created by the Relationship.sbs Team. We publish practical tools and concise guides to help people improve communication, build healthy boundaries, and understand attachment patterns in everyday relationships.

Suggestions, corrections, or collaboration: info@relationship.sbs

What we publish

Short tests

Lightweight quizzes to explore attachment, communication needs, and conflict style.

Actionable scripts

Practical wording for boundaries, repair attempts, apologies, and difficult conversations.

Focused articles

Single-topic posts with clear examples and next-step takeaways.

Editorial approach

  • Clarity over hype: practical guidance, no unrealistic promises.
  • Usefulness first: steps you can apply immediately.
  • Context matters: we avoid one-size-fits-all advice.
  • Continuous improvement: we refine content based on feedback and performance.

Note: Content is educational and not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or legal advice.
