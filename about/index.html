Who we are

We’re a small editorial team led by Laura Aram Smith, a psychologist. The site is designed for people who want clear relationship language and usable tools—not vague advice, hype, or one-size-fits-all rules.

Start here

  • Attachment Style Test — a quick starting point to understand patterns.
  • Love Language Test — explore what helps you feel cared for.
  • Conflict Style Test — understand how you argue and repair.
  • Text Decoder — help interpreting messages without spiraling.

Research & sources

Our content is informed by established relationship psychology concepts, including attachment theory, emotional regulation, boundary-setting, and conflict repair frameworks. We focus on practical guidance that can be used in real conversations.

Editorial approach

  • Clarity over hype: practical guidance, no unrealistic promises.
  • Usefulness first: steps you can apply immediately.
  • Context matters: we avoid one-size-fits-all advice.
  • Continuous improvement: we refine content based on feedback and performance.

Corrections & feedback

If you notice an error, want a clarification, or would like to suggest an improvement, email us at info@relationship.sbs. We review feedback and update content when needed.

Note: Content is educational and not a substitute for professional medical, psychological, or legal advice. If you are experiencing abuse, threats, or severe distress, please contact local emergency services or a licensed professional in your area.
